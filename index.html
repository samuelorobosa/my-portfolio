ABOUT ME

Samuel Orobosa Amagbakhen is a creative front-end developer who is passionate about digital technology and its future in our present day world. He is also committed to positioning himself to become an agent of change and hopes to do this through continuous learning, community service and challenging tasks

As an active volunteer for Junior Chamber International,Nigeria(UNIBEN chapter) and a member of Facebook Developers Circle, Benin, Samuel, is committed to making the society better by joining projects and programs geared towards community building.

He holds certifications in Responsive Web Design from freeCodeCamp and is currently undergoing his bachelor's degree program in Computer Science at The University of Benin.

With high proficiency in oral and written communication, he loves creating inspiring contents. Fueled by his boundless enthusiasm, he loves to act on his ideas and bring them to life. He has great stress management and time management skills and continues to show this through his ability to handle pressure and meet deadlines.

He is a great people person and is easily inspired. When he isn't coding, he's thinking of his next idea, listening to music, studying or playing basketball.
